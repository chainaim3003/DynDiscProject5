Traction

Built on open standards, advised by the people who created them, and recognized by the institutions that govern trusted finance and identity — for a pre-revenue venture, this standards backing is our strongest credibility signal.
Standards alignment & institutional relationships

Built natively on open standards rather than proprietary formats: GLEIF vLEI / LEI (legal-entity identity), ACTUS (financial-contract risk), OMG BPMN 2.0 (business process), DCSA (electronic bill of lading), and PEPPOL (e-invoicing).

Founder is an advisor to the ACTUS Financial Working Group and to CRAFT (Center for Advancing Research in Financial Technology). ACTUS co-founder Dr. Willi Brammertz (Chair, ACTUS Users Association Board of Governors) is a project advisor.

ZK-PRET reviewed and FUNDED by the Mina Foundation (Navigator grant).

Aligned with the GLEIF vLEI ecosystem and the Cardano Foundation's Veridian platform for legal-entity identity.
